Chapter 0: Enforcement Report

Released in the archive.

Cycle 230. Day 017. 2:16 AM. Intruders are caught in the server room of a BatteryCorp corporate-class carrier vessel. At 2:30 AM, the carrier ship sustains extreme structural damage and is no longer able to maintain flight. Seven thousand of the ten thousand servers on board sustain irreparable damage. It takes three hours before server performance in sector 15 restores. Four of the intruders are killed. The survivor is arrested, and charged with interference and espionage of Power City servers. Sentenced to VoidGate prison for life. It is of note this vessel was carrying ████████████ at the time. ████████████ was neither located by insurgents nor impacted during the crash, and was later retrieved by enforcement officers.

Cycle 230. Day 104. 4:42 AM. An internal alarm is tripped at a BatteryCorp Research Facility. The alarm had been reported faulty for months and is dismissed by security staff. At 5:00 an individual is detained for failing to procure proper BatteryCorp identification at an internal checkpoint. On their person are multiple high-risk documents taken from the facility’s archives. Multiple of these documents are damaged in the ensuing altercation. Enforcement arrives at 5:13 AM to apprehend the individual. They were sentenced to VoidGate prison for life. Fifteen members of the BatteryCorp Research Facility security staff were removed from their positions. Surviving documents were returned. One document found on the person of interest had not been taken from the archives, and spoke of the existence of ████████████. This document was promptly destroyed.

Cycle 230. Day 291. 6:00 PM. Security drones alert enforcement of multiple red-class individuals entering an apartment in sector 3. At 6:05, power meters monitoring sector 3 record a red-class power spike. At 6:15 PM, the apartment is raided. Multiple attuned artifacts are confiscated, including ████████████. Enforcers at the scene mention what appears to be a “procedure of ritualistic nature.” Nineteen individuals are arrested for occultish power manipulation and disruption of peace. Eighteen are sent to various medium-security containment facilities around Power City. One is sent to VoidGate prison for life.

Cycle 231. Day 005. 3:59 AM. Enforcements receive reports of power drainage from multiple individuals in sector 15. Enforcement notes interference from the ████████████ as a possible cause, and the ████████████ unit is deployed, consisting of fifteen Blue-class enforcers, three Red-class enforcers, and ████████████. Security drones from sectors 14 and 16 arrive in sector 15 to provide increased surveillance. At 5:22 AM an individual is apprehended at a transport dock, and suspected interference from ████████████ is dismissed. On the individual’s person are multiple batteries, as well as a unique tertiary artifact. The batteries are discarded. The artifact is confiscated upon the individual’s arrival to VoidGate. They are given a life sentence.

Cycle 231. Day 142. 9:12 AM. Sensors in sector 10 detect the second largest power spike recorded to date. Approximately 12% of all infrastructure in sector 10 is decimated. The fatality count is not calculated. Important BatteryCorp personnel including Doctor ████████████, ████████████, and Director ████████████ whose residences are recorded to have been destroyed have not made contact. Enforcements arriving at the scene find one survivor suffering from extreme power depletion. The individual is instantly transported to VoidGate prison with a life sentence. The public is informed the cause of the blast is a rare natural power surge.
